Cleff

Cleff is a social network for musicians. The app finds a users location and displays a stream of videos from musicians in their local area. Users essentially build a music resume and post a video of them playing to be found. The app also features two forums and a messaging system for communication which I built. This is the app I set out to build when I first started learning how to program and by the ninth week of the Iron Yard I felt I had the skills needed to build a prototype version, so I made it my final project.
